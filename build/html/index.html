I am a researcher with the Center for Marine Autonomy and Robotics at Virginia Tech, and I work under the supervision of Dr. Dan Stilwell.

I completed an M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering in 2018. In my thesis I derived a novel dynamic model for the Dragon autonmous underwater vehicle (AUV) coupled with its payload.

I am pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering with a research focus on collaborative subsea mapping. In particular, I seek to develop coordination strategies that optimize a rigorous measure of mission performance while requiring minimal communication.
